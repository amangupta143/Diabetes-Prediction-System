How It Works

Our diabetes prediction system uses a Random Forest machine learning model trained on medical data to assess your risk of diabetes based on various health metrics.

The model analyzes factors such as glucose levels, insulin, BMI, age, and other important health indicators to generate a personalized risk assessment.

With an accuracy of 74% and ROC AUC score of 82%, our model provides reliable predictions to help you understand your health better.
